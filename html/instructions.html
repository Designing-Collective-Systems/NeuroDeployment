Instructions

  1. The aim of this task is to slide your finger from one circle to another.
  2. Start the task by placing your finger on the circle with the number 1 in it.
  3. Slide your finger from 1 to the circle with the letter A. After this, go to the circle with the number 2.
  4. Continue moving in this pattern - number first, followed by a letter until you reach the end.
  5. Do not lift your finger from the screen while performing this task.
  6. You will be asked to repeat this task 5 times in total.
